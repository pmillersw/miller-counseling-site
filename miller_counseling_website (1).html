About Me

I’m Pam Miller, a Licensed Clinical Social Worker with over 20 years of experience supporting individuals through anxiety, depression, and emotional regulation. My approach is grounded in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), helping clients gain insight into their thoughts, emotions, and behaviors to build healthier coping strategies.
